Founder & Creative Director

Hey, I'm Jay.

Most podcasts don't struggle with ideas. They struggle with execution. Conversations run too long, pacing feels off, and the final output doesn't match the quality of what was actually said. A lot of good content gets lost in the process.

That's where I come in. I focus on post-production. Editing, structuring, and refining recordings so they land the way they're supposed to.

Over time, working across different kinds of projects made one thing clear. Small details make a big difference. Clean audio, tight pacing, and intentional cuts change how people experience the content.

Now, I run Story Signal with a small team, keeping the work consistent and focused on one thing. Turning raw recordings into something clear, polished, and ready to represent you well.

How we work here

The people behind the work matter. Editors and collaborators are given the space to focus and do their job properly.

The process is structured, timelines are realistic, and communication stays clear. This keeps the quality consistent without burning people out.

When the team is taken care of, the work holds up. That standard carries through to every project.
